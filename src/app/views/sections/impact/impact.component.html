Our Impact

Our work supports Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 3, 4, 5 and 17. With our holistic approach we build an ecosystem of change by working closely with our partners and improving women's health. In turn this creates a positive impact on the social and economic status of women in the Bungoma county in Kenya.
